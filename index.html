About Color Rush

Color Rush is a fast-paced game autorunner that combines thrilling parkour action with colorful challenges. Color Rush game is a perfect blend of speed, skill, and color-matching fun that keeps you on your toes. Let's play and challenge yourself in this game!

What is Color Rush game about?

Color Rush is a high-speed autorunner that blends parkour-style movement with vibrant color-matching mechanics. Players control a runner who must switch colors mid-sprint to match the platforms ahead, leaping over gaps and dodging black hazards along the way. With sleek visuals, smooth controls, and challenging objectives, the game tests your timing, reaction speed, and ability to adapt under pressure. Unlock new worlds, master precise jumps, and see how far your reflexes can take you in this thrilling arcade experience!

Outstanding Features

  • Fast-paced parkour mechanics
  • Unique color-switching gameplay
  • Smooth, vibrant graphics
  • Simple two-button controls
  • Challenging objectives and a progression system

How to Play Color Rush

  • Color Switch: Match your character’s color with platforms using the spacebar.
  • Jump Over Gaps: Tap the up arrow (or W) for short jumps, hold for higher jumps.
  • Avoid Obstacles: Stay away from black zones and moving hazards.
  • Complete Goals: Each level has 3 special objectives; completing them earns "chameleons" to unlock new worlds.

Game Controls

  • Up Arrow / W: Jump
  • Hold Up Arrow: Longer jump
  • Spacebar: Switch color
